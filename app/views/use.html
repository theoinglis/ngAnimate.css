How to Use

  • 1

  • Add the AngularJS ngAnimate script to your project

  • 2

  • Reference the 'ngAnimate' module as a dependency in your app

  • 3

  • Add one of the following files to your app:
    nga.css
    (leaner - simple)
    or
    nga.all.css
    (more customisable - advanced)

  • 4

  • Add the classes in the 'Classes Applied' section to the element you want to animate

  • Done
